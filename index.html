Quick answers

Does the magnet hold with a full bottle?
Yes. The magnetic base is strong enough to hold a full 24oz (700ml) bottle plus most phones without slipping on clean, flat steel surfaces.
Will it scratch my rack or phone?
No. The magnetic base has a protective coating, and the phone mount uses a smooth magnetic surface or included adhesive plate to avoid scratches.
Can I use hot liquids?
We recommend using the bottle for cold or room-temperature drinks. Hot liquids may create pressure inside and affect the flip lid seal.
What phones fit the holder?
Any MagSafe-compatible iPhone fits directly. For other phones or cases, use the included adhesive metal plate for a secure hold.
Is a shaker ball included?
No. Instead of a ball, the Strenvo bottle comes with a removable mesh strainer for smooth, lump-free shakes.
Can I remove the magnet?
Yes. The magnet is removable, so you can take it out for cleaning or if you prefer using the bottle without it.
Does it work on painted racks?
Yes, as long as the paint is thin and the rack is made of steel. Very thick coatings or rubberized finishes can reduce magnetic strength.
