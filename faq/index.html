Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about the Strenvo magnetic shaker, mounting, phone compatibility, care, and more.

1. What is the Strenvo Mag Shaker Bottle?

The Strenvo mag shaker bottle is a 700ml / 24oz BPA-free protein shaker with built-in magnetic mounts. It lets you stick your bottle securely to gym racks, benches, or lockers so you don’t have to leave it on dirty floors.

2. How is this different from other shaker bottles?

Unlike regular shakers, Strenvo is a mag shaker with magnetic sides. It also includes a phone holder mount and a hidden card slot features that make it more versatile for workouts, training, and travel.

3. Does it really stick securely in the gym?

Yes. The bottle has two integrated magnets designed to hold firmly on racks, benches, and lockers. It’s strong enough to stay put during workouts, but still easy to remove when you’re done.

4. Can it hold my phone?

Yes. Every Strenvo bottle comes with a metal adhesive plate that you can attach to your phone (if it’s not MagSafe already). This lets you use the bottle as a phone mount for tracking workouts, recording training videos, or hands-free calls.

5. Is the Strenvo mag shaker leak-proof?

Absolutely. The flip lid seals tightly, and the BPA-free body has been tested to prevent leaks, so you can carry it in your gym bag with confidence.

6. Does it include a mixing ball?

No. Instead of a ball, Strenvo uses a built-in mesh insert that breaks up clumps silently and smoothly, giving you lump-free shakes without the rattling noise.

7. How much liquid does it hold?

The bottle holds 700ml / 24oz, the ideal size for protein shakes, pre-workout, or hydration during exercise.

8. Is it BPA-free and safe to use?

Yes. The Strenvo magnetic gym shaker is made from BPA-free, food-safe plastic with a silicone seal to keep drinks safe and your bag dry.

9. Is it dishwasher safe?

Yes, it is top-rack dishwasher safe. However, avoid using high-temperature water to wash, as it may reduce the lifespan of the lid and magnetic components.

10. Does it work with MagSafe phones?

Yes. If your phone is MagSafe compatible, you can attach it directly. If not, we include an adhesive metal plate so any phone can attach securely.

11. What colors are available?

Right now, Strenvo is available in matte black, a sleek and stylish look that fits every gym environment. More colors are planned for the future.

12. Who is this bottle for?

It’s designed for gym-goers, athletes, and fitness enthusiasts who want a clean, clutter-free workout space. It’s also perfect for travelers or anyone who wants a smarter shaker bottle.

13. Does it come with a warranty?

Yes. We stand by Strenvo with a satisfaction guarantee. If you experience issues with performance or quality, contact us and we’ll make it right.

14. Can I gift this to someone?

Definitely. Strenvo makes a great gift for men and women who train, travel, or just love smart gym gear.
